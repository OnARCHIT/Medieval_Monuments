Airavatesvara Temple, Darasuram

#temple #chola #granite #UNESCO #civilengineering

Location & Period

Location: Darasuram
Dynasty: Chola (12th century)
Deity: Shiva

History

  • Built by Rajaraja Chola II.
  • Recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Architecture

  • Chariot-shaped mandapa and musical steps.
  • Multi-tiered vimana with intricate carvings.

Civil Engineering & Materials

  • Constructed with granite and sandstone on a wide shallow base.
  • Cantilevered steps designed for musical resonance.
  • Rainwater channels protect the foundation.

Builders’ Thought Process

  • Granite used for durability, sandstone for detailed carving.
  • Musical steps innovated for ritual acoustics and interaction.
