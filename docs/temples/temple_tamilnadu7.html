Kapaleeswarar Temple, Chennai

#temple #pallava #vijayanagara #civilengineering

Location & Dynasty

Location: Mylapore, Chennai
Dynasty: Pallava, rebuilt by Vijayanagara
Deity: Shiva

History

  • Original temple (7th century) destroyed by Portuguese, rebuilt in 16th century.

Architecture

  • 37m gopuram, pillared halls, temple tank.
  • Dravidian style, intricate stucco figures.

Civil Engineering & Materials

  • Granite and brick, lime mortar.
  • Tank for water management and rituals.

Builders’ Thought Process

  • Rebuilt for urban religious life.
  • Gopuram as city landmark.

Modern Comparison

Feature Traditional Modern RCC
Landmark Value High (gopuram) RCC less iconic

Unique Features

  • Major city festival: Panguni Peruvizha.
