Pamban Bridge (Old & New)

#bridge #railway #sea #civilengineering #pamban

Location & Period

Location: Connects Mandapam (mainland) to Rameswaram Island
Built: 1911–1914 (original), 2020–2024 (new)
Type: Railway sea bridge

History

  • 1914 Bridge: First sea bridge in India with manually operated Scherzer rolling lift span.
  • Survived the 1964 cyclone; decommissioned in 2022.
  • 2024 Bridge: India’s first vertical lift railway bridge; inaugurated April 2025.

Architecture & Civil Engineering

  • Original: 2.06 km, 143 piers, steel on concrete, 19m elevation, manual lift span.
  • New: 2.07 km, vertical lift, double track, 22m clearance, corrosion-resistant coatings.
  • Designed for climate resilience and minimal maintenance.

Builders' Thought Process

  • 1914: Connected pilgrims and trade with Rameswaram; built to endure marine environment.
  • 2024: Emphasized modernization, durability, high-speed rail, and automation.
