Kaveri River Bridges (Trichy–Srirangam)

#bridge #kaveri #civilengineering

Location & Type

Location: Trichy, spanning the Kaveri and Kollidam rivers
Type: Historic masonry and steel bridges

History & Engineering

  • Multiple bridges connect Srirangam temple island to the mainland.
  • Oldest are masonry arches; later ones are steel truss and concrete.

Unique Features

  • Vital for connecting the Srirangam temple complex and city.
  • Blend of colonial and modern engineering.
