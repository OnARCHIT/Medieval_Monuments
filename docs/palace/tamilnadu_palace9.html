Ramalinga Vilasam Palace, Ramanathapuram

#palace #sethupathi #ramanathapuram #civilengineering

Location & Dynasty

Location: Ramanathapuram
Built: 17th–18th centuries
Dynasty: Sethupathi

History

  • Seat of the Sethupathi kings of Ramnad, who controlled the southern Tamil coast.
  • Still partly occupied by the royal family; parts open as a museum.

Architecture

  • Large halls, pillared corridors, open courtyards, and a Durbar Hall.
  • Painted ceilings, murals, and royal artifacts.

Civil Engineering & Materials

  • Materials: Brick, lime mortar, teak wood.
  • Design: High ceilings, thick walls for cooling, courtyards for ventilation.

Builders’ Thought Process

  • Designed for ceremonial, residential, and administrative functions.
  • Used local materials for climate and cost.
