Thenkaraikottai Fort

#fort #vijayanagara #civilengineering

Location & Era

Location: Krishnagiri District, Tamil Nadu
Built: Vijayanagara Empire (by Seelappa Nayakkar & Chennappa Nayakkar)

History

  • The only major land fort in Tamil Nadu, built for tax collection and military control[1][3].
  • Contains Sri Kalyana Ramaswamy temple, granaries, stables, cannons, palace hall, and more[1][3].

Architecture

  • Spans 16 hectares (40 acres) with granite and brick walls[1][3][6].
  • Features musical pillars, Ramayana and Mahabharata sculptures, temple tank and well[1][3][6].

Civil Engineering & Materials

  • Materials: Granite, brick, lime mortar[1][3].
  • Walls: Some ruined, restoration uses traditional lime mortar and bricks[1][3].
  • Water Management: Temple tank and well[1][3].

Builders’ Thought Process

  • Granite and brick for local availability and defense[1][3].
  • Integrated temple and fort for community and military use[1][3].

Unique Features

  • Musical pillars, palace hall, temple tank, and Vaishnavite sculptures[1][3][6].
