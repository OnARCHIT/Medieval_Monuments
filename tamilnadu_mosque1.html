Bahram Jung Mosque, Nandanam, Chennai

#mosque #arcotnawab #civilengineering

Location & Construction

Location: Nandanam, Chennai
Built: 1795 CE

History

  • Built by Bahram Jung, a noble in the court of the Nawab of Arcot.
  • Important for its Indo-Islamic architectural features.

Architecture

  • Style: Indo-Islamic, with a prominent dome and minaret.
  • Plan: Spacious prayer hall, courtyard, and tank.

Civil Engineering & Materials

  • Brick and lime mortar: Used for main structure.
  • Dome: Plaster and lime finish for waterproofing.

Builders’ Thought Process

  • Built for the growing Muslim community in colonial Madras.

Modern Comparison

Feature Traditional (Brick/Lime) Modern RCC
Dome Plaster, lime RCC, steel frame

Unique Features

  • Heritage site, still active for worship.
