Our Lady of Snows Basilica, Thoothukudi

#church #basilica #portuguese #civilengineering

Location & Era

Location: Thoothukudi (Tuticorin)
Built: 16th century (Portuguese, rebuilt 18th–20th c.)
Denomination: Roman Catholic

History

  • Built by Portuguese missionaries, expanded and rebuilt over centuries.
  • Famous for the annual “Our Lady of Snows” festival.

Architecture

  • European-style white façade, twin towers, and central dome.
  • Large nave, side chapels, and bell tower.

Civil Engineering & Materials

  • Brick and lime mortar.
  • Designed for monsoon resilience and large crowds.

Builders’ Thought Process

  • Built for Marian devotion and mass gatherings.
  • Designed for visibility and capacity.

Modern Comparison

Feature Traditional (Brick/Lime) Modern RCC
Durability High (with repairs) RCC, more modular

Unique Features

  • Annual festival draws thousands; processions and rituals.
