Vattakottai Fort

#fort #travancore #granite #coastal #civilengineering

Location & Era

Location: Kanyakumari
Built: 18th century (Travancore, by Marthanda Varma; expanded by De Lannoy)

History

  • Built by Marthanda Varma as a coastal defense.
  • Later expanded by De Lannoy.

Architecture

  • Circular (Vatta = round) granite fort, sea-facing.
  • Watchtowers, bastions, and ramparts.

Civil Engineering & Materials

  • Materials: Granite, lime mortar.
  • Walls: Thick, curved for wave resistance.
  • Foundation: On the beach, with sea wall.

Builders’ Thought Process

  • Granite for wave and cannon resistance.
  • Designed for 360° defense and sea surveillance.

Feature Traditional Modern RCC

Unique Features

  • Panoramic sea and Western Ghats views.
