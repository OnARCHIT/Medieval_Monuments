Ancient Period (300-600 CE)

Materials: Stone, Granite, Sandstone
Techniques: Precise cutting, Interlocking joints
Innovations: Mathematical precision, Astronomical alignment

Key Innovations

  • Advanced stone-cutting techniques
  • Complex geometric patterns
  • Earthquake-resistant construction
  • Astronomical observatories

Medieval Indian Architecture (600-1200 CE)

• Regional temple styles flourished
• Monumental stone temple complexes
• Distinctive Nagara, Dravida, and Vesara styles
• Advanced sculptural ornamentation

Key Innovations

  • Development of towering shikharas and vimanas
  • Elaborate mandapa (pillared hall) construction
  • Sophisticated drainage and ventilation systems
  • Integration of astronomy in temple orientation
