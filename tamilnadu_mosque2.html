Jama Masjid, Salem

#mosque #mughal #civilengineering

Location & Era

Location: Salem
Built: 17th century (during Mughal emperor Aurangzeb’s reign)

History

  • One of the oldest mosques in Tamil Nadu, built under Mughal patronage.
  • Important religious and cultural center in Salem.

Architecture

  • Style: Mughal-Indian fusion, grand arches, intricate carvings, symmetrical layout.
  • Features: Large prayer hall, minaret with city views, gardens.

Civil Engineering & Materials

  • Materials: Brick, lime mortar, stone.
  • Minaret: Designed for structural stability and city surveillance.
  • Gardens: Provide cooling and tranquility.

Builders’ Thought Process

  • Combined Islamic and local architectural traditions.
  • Designed for beauty, community, and resilience.
