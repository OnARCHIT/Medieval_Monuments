Kattabomman Fort (Panchalankurichi Fort)

#fort #kattabomman #civilengineering

Location & Era

Location: Thoothukudi District
Built: 17th–18th century (Veerapandiya Kattabomman)

History

  • Built by the legendary freedom fighter Veerapandiya Kattabomman.
  • Site of resistance against the British East India Company.

Architecture

  • Small, square fort with mud and brick walls, later rebuilt in stone.
  • Contains memorial hall, museum, and statue.

Civil Engineering & Materials

  • Mud, brick, later stone.
  • Thick, low walls designed for local defense.
  • Well and tank inside.

Builders’ Thought Process

  • Quick, local construction for guerrilla resistance.

Modern Comparison

Feature Traditional (Mud/Brick) Modern RCC
Defense Local, temporary RCC, more durable

Unique Features

  • Memorial to Kattabomman, site of annual festival.
