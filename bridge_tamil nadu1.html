Annai Indira Gandhi Road Bridge (Pamban Road Bridge)

#bridge #road #sea #civilengineering

Location & Overview

  • Location: Parallel to Pamban Rail Bridge, Rameswaram
  • Built: 1988
  • Type: Road bridge, concrete

History & Engineering

  • Built to provide a road link to Rameswaram, parallel to the rail bridge.
  • 2.345 km long, 72 spans, 13.5m above sea level.
  • Pre-stressed concrete, designed for high winds and salt spray.

Unique Features

  • Only road link to Rameswaram.
  • Scenic drive, featured in films and tourism campaigns.
