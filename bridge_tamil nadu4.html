Mettur Dam Bridge

#bridge #dam #mettur #civilengineering

Location & Period

Location: Salem District
Built: 1934
Type: Road and utility bridge atop dam

History

  • Built as part of the Mettur Dam project, one of the largest in India.
  • Spans the Kaveri River, vital for irrigation and power.

Architecture & Civil Engineering

  • Concrete and steel, integral with dam superstructure.
  • Designed for flood resilience and heavy loads.

Builders’ Thought Process

  • Combined dam and bridge for multi-use infrastructure.
  • Focused on longevity and flood safety.

Unique Features

  • Part of a major irrigation and hydroelectric project.
  • Scenic views, vital for local transport.
