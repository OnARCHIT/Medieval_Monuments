Coleroon Bridge (Upper Anaicut Bridge), near Srirangam

#bridge #coleroon #anaicut #civilengineering

Location & Overview

  • Location: Near Srirangam, Trichy District
  • Built: 19th century (Sir Arthur Cotton)
  • Type: Masonry and steel

History

  • Built by British engineer Sir Arthur Cotton as part of the Kaveri delta irrigation system.
  • Connects Srirangam with the northern bank of the Coleroon (Kollidam) River.

Architecture & Civil Engineering

  • Masonry arches and steel superstructure.
  • Built to withstand strong river currents and monsoon floods.

Builders’ Thought Process

  • Used local stone for piers and imported steel for spans.
  • Designed for durability and low maintenance.

Unique Features

  • Part of one of India’s earliest large-scale irrigation networks.
  • Still in use for rail and road traffic.
