Sadras Dutch Fort

#fort #dutch #coastal #civilengineering

Location & Era

Location: Kalpakkam, near Mahabalipuram
Built: 17th century (Dutch)

History

  • Originally a Hindu temple site, converted by the Dutch for trade.
  • Used for exporting textiles, salt, and other goods.

Architecture

  • High brick walls, cemetery, secret tunnel.
  • Ruins of halls, bastions, and warehouses.

Civil Engineering & Materials

  • Materials: Brick, lime mortar.
  • Walls: High, thick, with Dutch military architecture features.

Builders’ Thought Process

  • Designed for trade protection and coastal defense.
  • Used local materials for speed and cost.
